My name is Sanjeev—though I’m often known as San—and I serve as a Senior Pipeline Developer at Skydance Animation in Madrid. I am rather partial to Linus Torvalds’ minimalist philosophy; I have, on occasion, applied it within my software development practice and found it remarkably advantageous, though embracing minimalism in everyday life proves decidedly challenging. By nature, I am a sceptical atheist—outgoing, enthusiastic, and somewhat nyctophilic. I am an adherent of Carl Sagan’s worldview and a proponent of transhumanism. In my spare time, I am an avid reader, gravitating particularly towards non-fiction works on psychology, astronomy, or genetics.
